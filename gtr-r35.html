The Origin Idea

The R35 was envisioned as a "multi-performance supercar." The goal was to build an absolute track weapon capable of outperforming elite European supercars, while remaining accessible, reliable, and comfortable enough for daily road driving.

The Creators

Spearheaded by legendary Chief Engineer Kazutoshi Mizuno ("Mr. GT-R") and Design Director Shiro Nakamura. Each VR38DETT engine was entirely hand-built by one of only nine master "Takumi" craftsmen at Nissan's facility in Yokohama.

Production Era

Proudly manufactured at Nissan's high-tech plant in Tochigi, Japan. The R35 enjoyed an uncommonly long and dominant 18-year production run beginning in late 2007, before officially wrapping up production in August 2025.

The Company

Founded originally in 1933 by Yoshisuke Aikawa in Japan, Nissan Motor Co. has grown into a global automotive titan. Today, it operates at the highest levels of performance engineering under the Renault-Nissan-Mitsubishi Alliance.
